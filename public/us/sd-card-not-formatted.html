DO NOT CLICK "FORMAT DISK" - YOUR DATA IS STILL THERE

"You need to format the disk in drive" Error Fix

This message appears when the file system (FAT32/exFAT) of your SD card becomes RAW. It means the camera or computer can no longer read the partition table, but the RAW data blocks containing your photos and videos are intact.

1
Disconnect: Remove the card from the camera or drone immediately.
2
Scan: Use a sector-by-sector scan to rebuild the directory tree.
3
Recover: Export files to your local drive before reformatting the card.
