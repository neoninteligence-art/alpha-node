BitLocker Locked Drive Access

Security Notice: Encryption software protects data at the volume level. If the recovery key is missing due to a BIOS update or hardware change, standard OS tools will fail to unlock the drive.

Our data recovery protocol utilizes low-level sector mapping to identify BitLocker metadata. While it cannot "crack" the AES-256 encryption, it can bypass system-level lockouts to scan for accessible data clusters if the drive was partially decrypted or the header is intact.

  • Detection: Identifying BitLocker-To-Go and enterprise encrypted volumes.
  • Cloning: Creating a bit-by-bit image of the encrypted drive to prevent data decay.
  • Extraction: Using the deep-scan engine to find files within the NTFS partition structure.
